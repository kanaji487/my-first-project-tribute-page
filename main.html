History

  • 10 Sep 2019 - she announced on her Twitter that she would be getting her 3D model
  • 14 Sep 2019 - she debuted her 3D model during a stream.
  • 26 Dec 2019 - she published on Twitter that she would get her second 2D costume. This costume was later revealed that day during a stream, and features a mostly brown dress with a ribbon and a hat.
  • 01 Jan 2020 - she debuted her third 2D costume, a kimono in celebration of the new year. The outfit also comes with a ridiculous kadomatsu-shaped hat, with three bamboo stems that can bob up and down.
  • 24 Jan 2020 - along with the other hololive girls up to the third generation, she debuted her 3D idol outfit at hololive's 1st fes. Nonstop Story.
  • 01 Nov 2020 - Korone became the first hololive VTuber in the Japanese branch to reach 1 million YouTube subscribers.

Event

  • 28 Jan 2020 it was announced on Dengekionline that she would made an appearance in the game Neptunia Virtual Stars as one of the VTuber guest characters alongside Tokino Sora, Roboco, Sakura Miko, Shirakami Fubuki, Minato Aqua, Yuzuki Choco, Shirogane Noel and Houshou Marine./li>
  • 03 Jul 2020 - Korone announced on her Twitter account that she would appear in a collaboration with CyberStep's online crane game Toreba, where cushions with her image can be obtained, available until units run out.
